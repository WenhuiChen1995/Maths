Wenhui Chen (陈文辉)

Associate Professor at School of Mathematics and Information Science, Guangzhou University

Email: wenhui.chen.math "at" gmail.com

Address: Guangzhou University, No. 230 West Waihuan Road, Higher Education Mega Center, 510006 Guangzhou, China

Research field: Nonlilnear Partial Differential Equations

Research interest: I have been working on nonlinear evolution equations, for example, semilinear damped waves, thermoelastic systems and several fundamental models in nonlinear acoustics. The major concern in my research is qualitative properties of solutions, including global (in time) well-posedness, blow-up, lifespan estimates, large-time properties (e.g. optimal decay/growth estimates), asymptotic profiles (e.g. optimal leading terms), singular limits, propagation of singularities and smoothing effect.
